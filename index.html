Website Asignment

For this assignment you may work in group of three or less, or you may work individually. The final deliverable will be a website for a fictional small business that your group creates. This assignment will be very valueable to you (and your resume); it is therrefore advised to take the to design it proffessionally.

Deliverable

  • Design mockup
    • You may use any number of tools, including
      • powerpoint
      • Lucidchart
      • pencil
      • gliffy
  • HTML source code (Three .html files)
    • You should create your HTML and CSS in a text editor. Recommended editiors are:
      • Windows: Notepad++
      • Mac: Sublimetext or textwragler
  • CSS source code (one .css file)
  • Screenshort of working website with atleast three different pages
  • Presentation of your worker website

Things I am Looking for

  • A main "landing" page, with company information
  • A navigation section with links to at least two other pages
    • For example: Home, About, Contact
  • Content on your pages: images, text, etc.; be creative.
  • Good basic design.
  • Well designed code:
    • Structural elements should be written in HTML
    • Design elements should be written in CSS
